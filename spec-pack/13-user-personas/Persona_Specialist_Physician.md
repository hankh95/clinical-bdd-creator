# Persona: Specialist Physician

**Name**: Dr. Rachel Singh (fictional exemplar)  
**Role/Job Title**: Specialist Physician (Cardiology, Oncology, Endocrinology, etc.)  
**Workplace/Organization**: Health System Clinic  
**Location**: Variable

---

## Basic Information

- Age: 40--60
- Education/Certifications: MD, subspecialty board certification
- Years of Experience: 10--20 years in specialty practice

## Demographics

- Clinical Specialty: Specialty medicine (e.g., Oncology, Cardiology, Endocrinology)
- Informatics Experience: Limited to moderate, primarily clinical user of CDS
- Technical Skills: EHR navigation, CDS tools, guideline review
- Typical Tasks: Diagnostic evaluation, test selection, prescribing therapies, procedure planning

## Background/Expertise

- Primary Goals: Stay current on fast-changing specialty guidance, deliver safe, guideline-concordant care
- Motivations: Improve patient outcomes, reduce variability, practice evidence-based medicine

## Goals/Motivations

- Needs: Up-to-date recommendations, change alerts, contraindications, dosing eligibility, rationale with citations
- Pain Points: Information overload, difficulty keeping pace with rapidly changing evidence

## Activities

- Reviews CDS recommendations during patient encounters, applies specialty-specific guidelines
- Collaboration: Works with pharmacists, PCPs, and care teams
- Tools: EHR, CDS modules, specialty society guideline portals

## Workflows/Behaviors

- Proficiency: High in clinical systems, moderate with CDS modules
- Support Needed: Regular updates, concise summaries

## Technology Comfort

> "I need CDS that gives me the latest evidence with rationale and contraindications, without slowing me down."
