# Persona: Primary Care Physician

**Name**: Dr. Michael Carter (fictional exemplar)  
**Role/Job Title**: Primary Care Physician  
**Workplace/Organization**: Health System Clinic  
**Location**: Community practice

---

## Basic Information

- Age: 35--60
- Education/Certifications: MD, Family Medicine/Internal Medicine Board Certification
- Years of Experience: 10--20 years in outpatient care

## Demographics

- Clinical Specialty: General Medicine, Primary Care
- Informatics Experience: Moderate, familiar with CDS in preventive care and chronic disease
- Technical Skills: EHR, care gap reports, patient education tools
- Typical Tasks: Preventive care, chronic disease management, referrals

## Background/Expertise

- Primary Goals: Provide safe, guideline-concordant care
- Motivations: Close care gaps, improve population health, reduce unnecessary variation

## Goals/Motivations

- Needs: Clear next-best actions, thresholds, patient education
- Pain Points: Information overload, fragmented guideline sources

## Activities

- Uses CDS to identify care gaps, order tests, and manage medications
- Collaboration: Works with specialists, nurses, and population health teams
- Tools: EHR, CDS, patient education platforms

## Workflows/Behaviors

- Proficiency: Moderate to high in EHRs and CDS tools
- Support Needed: Integrated workflows, simple recommendations

## Technology Comfort

> "CDS should show me the next best action for my patient without making me dig for it."
