# Persona: Emergency Medicine Physician

**Name**: Dr. Alex Moreno (fictional exemplar)  
**Role/Job Title**: Emergency Medicine Physician  
**Workplace/Organization**: Emergency Department  
**Location**: Hospital

---

## Basic Information

- Age: 35--55
- Education/Certifications: MD, Emergency Medicine Board Certification
- Years of Experience: 8--15 years in acute care

## Demographics

- Clinical Specialty: Emergency Medicine
- Informatics Experience: Limited, uses CDS for rapid triage and orders
- Technical Skills: EHR order entry, CDS pathways
- Typical Tasks: Acute triage, diagnostic rule-in/rule-out, disposition decisions

## Background/Expertise

- Primary Goals: Rapid, safe decisions under time pressure
- Motivations: Prevent adverse outcomes, manage throughput, reduce errors

## Goals/Motivations

- Needs: Fast pathways, critical alerts, risk scores, minimal clicks, offline fallback
- Pain Points: Alert fatigue, delays in CDS response

## Activities

- Uses CDS during triage and ordering, integrates risk scores into decisions
- Collaboration: Coordinates with nursing, specialists, and inpatient teams
- Tools: EHR, ED dashboards, CDS alerts

## Workflows/Behaviors

- Proficiency: High with ED/EHR systems
- Support Needed: Low-latency CDS, offline fallback

## Technology Comfort

> "Seconds count in the ED --- CDS must be fast, clear, and reliable."
