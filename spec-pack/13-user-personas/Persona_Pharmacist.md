# Persona: Pharmacist

**Name**: Dr. Laura Chen (fictional exemplar)  
**Role/Job Title**: Clinical Pharmacist  
**Workplace/Organization**: Health System  
**Location**: Hospital/Clinic Pharmacy

---

## Basic Information

- Age: 30--50
- Education/Certifications: PharmD, Board Certified Pharmacotherapy Specialist (BCPS)
- Years of Experience: 5--15 years in clinical pharmacy

## Demographics

- Clinical Specialty: Clinical Pharmacy
- Informatics Experience: High, daily user of medication CDS and formulary systems
- Technical Skills: RxNorm, drug interaction databases, dose calculators
- Typical Tasks: Validates medication orders, monitors for interactions, advises clinicians

## Background/Expertise

- Primary Goals: Safe, effective medication therapy plans
- Motivations: Prevent adverse drug events, optimize pharmacotherapy

## Goals/Motivations

- Needs: Accurate RxNorm mapping, dose calculators, interaction rules
- Pain Points: Alert fatigue, incomplete drug databases

## Activities

- Reviews orders, intervenes on dosing and interactions, educates clinicians
- Collaboration: Works closely with physicians and nurses
- Tools: EHR pharmacy modules, CDS, drug knowledge bases

## Workflows/Behaviors

- Proficiency: High with pharmacy informatics systems
- Support Needed: Customizable alerts, reliable mapping

## Technology Comfort

> "I need CDS that catches dangerous interactions but doesn't drown me in noise."
